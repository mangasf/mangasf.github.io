The Project

The Mangasf Project was born with the intention of helping companies to solve their problems by building technological solutions. For this, the project relies on a group of professionals with extensive experience in the IT sector. This group of professionals is led by engineer Fran Mangas.

The essence of the service offered by the project is to provide a comprehensive consulting model. This service covers from the support for the construction of equipment to the accompaniment in the implementation of solutions.

All our services are developed putting our users at the center of value delivery, adapting our processes and solutions to the needs of our customers at all times.

Who is Fran Mangas?

Computer Engineer specialized in computer science and web programming performance. I have studied at the UC (University of Cantabria) and at the Ramon Llull University of Barcelona.

I have been linked to the IT sector for the last 10 years, working in different types of industries. I have always held development and technical leadership roles from the early stages of biotech startups, from consulting to end product companies.

My career in the private sector has always been linked parallel to teaching and entrepreneurship. I collaborate with the University of La Salle in the delivery of post-graduate programs and lead my own technology consulting project.

I am passionate about teamwork, project and people management, and above all, focusing on software engineering as a way to solve complex software problems a way to make an impact.
